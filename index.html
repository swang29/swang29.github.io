Shubo Wang

I am a researcher at Nano and Molecular Systems research unit(NANOMO) in University of Oulu , Finland. My research interests are to gain deeper insights into processing-microstructure-properties correlations in nanomaterials and metallic materials by employing multi-scale electron microscopies (SEM, TEM, etc,), in/ex situ and operando synchrotron scattering and spectroscopy techniques. Involved topics are:

  • Physical metallurgy and surface treatment of metallic materials.
  • Functional photocatalytic materials and device.
  • Synchrotron X-ray scattering and spectroscopy characterizations.
  • Rechargeable Al/Na/Li ion batteries.
  • Interactions between hydrogen and materials.
